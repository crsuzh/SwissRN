Reminder:

SwissRN Annual Meeting and Swiss Reproducibility Award, 13.1.2023

The SwissRN Annual Meeting will take place in person on January 13, 2023 in Bern. It is a great opportunity to connect and discuss the activities of SwissRN and its future strategy.

Location: University of Bern, Hochschulstrasse 4, Kuppelraum

Program:

Time Topic
09:30-09:40 Welcome Note by VR Research Prof. Hugues Abriel
09:40-10:40 Presentation and Discussion of SwissRN Strategy
10:40-11:00 Coffee break
11:00-11:40 Reports from local nodes and discussion
11:40-12:00 Report SwissRN academy
12:00-13:30 Lunch break
13:30-14:00 Reports from working groups
14:00-14:45 Ceremony Swiss Reproducibility Award 2022. The winners are:
  • Category Biology and Medicine: Mathieu Seppey, University of Geneva
  • Category Humanities and Social Science: Caro Hautekiet and
    Tobia Spampatti, both University of Geneva
14:45-15:00 Coffee break
15:00-16:00 Keynote lecture by Brian Nosek, Co-founder and Executive Director
of the Center for Open Science:
“Sustaining the open science movement: Assets and risks”
16:00-16:15 General Assembly
